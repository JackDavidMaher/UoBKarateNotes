\section*{}
Having been the General Secretary for the past year, I have seen the challenges and limitations we face when operating the club. I am aware of the huge commitment required of the president and believe I am capable of filling the role.
I would love to campaign saying that we will get new mats, that the BKO will be a Super Series event, and that UBSport will pay for all of this, but knowing that Becky, Adam, and Tom have all fought for these in the past, I know that they are not at all things that can be guaranteed.
\newline
\newline
What I can be confident in campaigning on is the fact that I will run the club with the same dedication and passion that I have shown as Secretary, and that I will continue to work tirelessly to improve the club and provide the best possible experience for all members across all aspects of the club, from training to competitions to social events.
\newline
\newline
You can expect me to be a President who listens to the concerns and ideas of all karate members and is committed to fostering a positive, safe, and inclusive club culture. 
I will work with the committee to ensure that we are making decisions that are in the best interest of the club and its members.
I will always be open to feedback and suggestions.
\newline
I am also very much aware that the committee is the sum of its parts, it is a team that is only as strong as the weakest link.
I don't want to be a president who tries to do everything themselves or who doesn't trust the committee members to do their jobs effectively.
I see it would be my responsibility to assist the committee in their roles so they can be as effective as possible through thorough planning and transparent communication.
I would always strive to create an environment where all members feel heard and where the president's role is to facilitate the work of the committee and not to dictate it.
\newline
\newline
Having been a member of the club for 3 years and serving as General Secretary for the past year, I know where attention needs to be paid and where the club can be improved, and I am confident that I have the experience and dedication to lead the club in a positive future.
\newline
Having served on the UBSport Club Executive Committee for the past year, I have gained valuable insight in club management and leadership. 
I am confident that I can apply this knowledge when leading the club as President when campaigning for karate's interests.
\newline
\newline
I hope that through my previous experience and dedication to the club that you have the confidence in me to vote for me as your next President.
\newline
\newline
\begin{flushright}
- Jack D. Maher
\end{flushright}
