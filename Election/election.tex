\section*{}
Having been the General Secretary for the past year, I have seen firsthand the challenges and limitations we face when operating the club, and I am not going to promise anything that I am not confident can be achieved.
I would love to campaign saying that we will get new mats, that the BKO will be a Super Series event, and that UBSport will pay for all of this, knowing that Becky, Adam, and Tom have all fought for these things in the past, I know that it is not something that can be guaranteed.
\newline
\newline
What I can be confident in campaigning on is the fact that I will run the club with the same dedication and passion that I have shown as General Secretary, and that I will continue to work tirelessly to improve the club and provide the best possible experience for all members across all aspects of the club, from training to competitions to social events.
\newline
\newline
You can expect me to be a President who listens to the concerns and ideas of all members and is committed to fostering a positive and inclusive club culture. 
I will work closely with the committee to ensure that we are making decisions that are in the best interest of the club and its members, and I will always be open to feedback and suggestions.
I am also very much aware that the committee is the sum of its parts and a dictatorial style of leadership is not something that I would ever want to implement. 
I would always strive to create an environment where all members feel heard and where the president's role is to facilitate the work of the committee and not to dictate it.
It would be my goal to assist the committee in their roles so they can be as effective as possible through effective planning and transparent communication.
